At Ember & Sage, we celebrate the timeless craft of wood-fired cooking, where the open flame transforms simple, fresh ingredients into bold, unforgettable flavors. Our culinary philosophy is rooted in honoring tradition while embracing creativity. Every dish is a reflection of the season’s best, thoughtfully paired with herbs like sage, thyme, and rosemary to enhance natural flavors. From the crackling embers of the hearth to the aromas that fill the air, we create an experience that is both comforting and refined.

Our name embodies our passion: “Ember” symbolizes the glowing warmth of the fire, where every dish begins its journey, while “Sage” represents wisdom, tradition, and the fragrant herb that adds depth to many of our creations. We craft meals that blend rustic charm with modern elegance, offering a menu that ranges from hearty ribeye steaks and buttery sweet potato gnocchi to delicate honey lavender panna cotta. Each plate is a tribute to the art of slow cooking, where time and care are the key ingredients.

The atmosphere at Ember & Sage is designed to evoke a sense of home—a cozy, inviting space where guests can relax and savor every moment. Natural wood accents, soft lighting, and the warmth of a wood-fired oven create an ambiance that is both intimate and lively. Whether you’re enjoying an intimate dinner for two or gathering with friends for a special celebration, our open kitchen invites you to witness the artistry and care behind each dish, fostering a connection between our chefs and guests.

More than just a restaurant, Ember & Sage is a place where community comes together. We believe in the power of food to connect, to heal, and to create lasting memories. Whether you’re celebrating life’s milestones or simply sharing a meal with loved ones, we aim to make every visit meaningful. Welcome to Ember & Sage—a place where fire meets flavor, tradition meets creativity, and every meal is a celebration of togetherness.
